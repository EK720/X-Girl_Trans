X-GIRL GENERAL TERM SHEET

Intelligence Division - Kiyomi's workplace and a branch of the military.

Internal Affairs Inspector - Katou's official job title.

Kiyomi is a Major. Anna is a Colonel, Katou is an Inspector and above her, she refers to him as Commander.

April, Lei-Fa, Nana, Mitsuko - Names of the 4 prisoners Kiyomi is tasked to catch. Lei-Fa is a romanisation of 雷發, meaning "to emit thunder". All their names are written in katakana.

Eriko (エリコ), Priss (プリス) - Additional heroines who haven't shown up yet.

Purple Rock (think it's slang) - a fictional drug that seems similar to opium. Translates from 紫岩.

Pod-Mod - A purely physical(?) cybernetic enhancement. Slang, translates from ポッドモッド.

Enhancement / mod (neuro-mod, phys-mod) - a cybernetic body modification, either physical or neural, to improve someone's capabilities. Translates from 肉体改造.

Cyber Socket / Socket - brain-computer interface port, translates from ソケット.

Chiba City - Black market district of the city? Translates from 千葉シティ.

Low-Tech - A person with none or very few enhancements. Kiyomi is one of these. Seen as uncommon, but not generally looked down on. Similar to "dirty" in Cyberpunk slang. Translates from ローテク.

High-Tech - A person who have enhancements, like Nana, Anna and Eriko. Translates from ニューテク. (Originally New-Tech)

Circuit-splicer, etc - terms for this world's ripperdocs. A doctor, official or not, who can install enhancements.

The System or A System - It's a system being developed in partnership between the military and a private corporation. Original translation is Mechanism = System (機構＝システム).

Sakata Conglomerate - Sakata Zaibatsu. Zaibatsu definition: A group of large conglomerates or large corporations. Translates from 坂田ザイバツ .

Sigmund Sakata - Eriko's father. Translates from サカタ・ジクムント.

Morii - The head of information guild. Translates from モリイ)
